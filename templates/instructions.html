Instructions for Taking the Leadership Assessment

This leadership assessment is designed to help you better understand your natural leadership tendencies and preferences. Your honest and thoughtful responses will provide the most accurate and useful results. Please read the following guidelines before you begin:

1. Be Honest

There are no right or wrong answers. The goal is to reflect your authentic self — not who you think you should be. Honest answers will result in the most valuable insights to help you grow as a leader.

2. Don't Overthink, but Don't Rush

Read each question carefully, but avoid spending too much time trying to find a "perfect" answer. Go with the response that feels most natural or describes how you typically behave.

3. Always and Never Are Rare — But Use Them if They Fit

While most situations are not black and white, if you feel that "always" or "never" truly applies to you for a certain question, don't be afraid to select it.

4. Choose a Quiet Environment

For best results, complete the assessment in a quiet, comfortable space with minimal distractions. This will allow you to focus and provide your most accurate responses.

5. Complete the Assessment in One Sitting

Set aside enough time to complete the assessment in one uninterrupted session. This ensures your responses reflect a consistent mindset.

6. Remember: There's No Perfect Leader

This assessment is not a test, and it does not measure success or failure. Different leadership styles are effective in different situations, and understanding your style is the first step to becoming an adaptable, self-aware leader.

7. Provide Feedback to Help Us Improve

After completing the assessment questions, there will be a few additional questions aimed at helping us improve the assessment process. Your feedback is invaluable, and we appreciate your time in completing this section.

Once you're ready, take a deep breath, relax, and begin.
